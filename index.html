My idea

Teach relationships like sports:
If we were coaching soccer, we would teach the rules, goals, field, players, and plays. We would practice the fundamental skills of soccer: passing the ball, kicking, dribbling, shooting...

Most sex educations teach injuries (STDs and pregnancy) and anatomy. Most sports don't cover every possible injury nor the anatomy of your body.

Why is that?
I believe in teaching relationships like we would teach a sport:

  • Practical frameworks (what is and isn't ok)
  • Drills of the fundamentals (communication skills)
  • Ways of getting in and out of relationships

Help and Teach Me

I plan to update this document and pass it on to a team who is passionate about getting accurate, practical advice and information.
